  • South American beans:

For example, in 2020, Brazil was the leading producer of coffee beans providing over a third of the global production. Also, Clomobian coffee is nothing to be unnoticed, producing approximatley 10% of the global production (Anshool Deshmukh, 2021).   • African & Arabian beans:

    • Eastren Africa and Arabia are the earliest coffee consumers. Thus, it is only logical for people to consider Kenyan and Ethiopian coffees to be "the world's finest gourmet coffees" (Espresso & Coffee Guide, 2011). The coffee beans from this area of the world varies in a lot of features. You can find maltitudes of scents, textures & shapes For example, when it comes to taste they tend to be fruity, floral, lemony and/or citrusy.
