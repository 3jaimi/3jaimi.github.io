Before we get started with things,

let's talk about coffee

"...The most widely consumed psychoactive substance on earth..."(New Scientific, 2005) can be found everywhere today. Your local shop, the biggest hypermarkets and fanciest hotels. Everywhere. But where did it originate from?

Coffee reached eurpoe during the 17th century. However, the origin of coffee belongs to the arabian peninsula; specifaclly, Yemen.
In the 15th century, an Ottoman solider found a drink with a weird odor and bitter taste. He found out the effects of that drink and took it
home (Turkey, nowdayas). Henceforth, it started being called "Kahveh". Furthermore, the Ottoman empire being a trading stop-point helped spread the
drink immensely. Reaching the land of pharoahs; after that, to the Netherlands, and finally, the United States.
